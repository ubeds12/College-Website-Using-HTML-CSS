Baliram Patil Mission Mandvi's S. N. Rai Inst. of Engg. & Tech. Poly Mahur , was established in the year 2010. The campus of the institute is spread across the 52 acres of land with academic and administrative building, Workshop, Girls Hostels, Boys Hostels, Faculty and staff quarters, Play Ground and Multi-Purpose Hall with Guest House.

The Institute is located at Pusad Road, in front of court Mahur, Mahur on the Bay of Penganga River on the west side of Mahur, 10 km from central bus stand.

There is one facility near the college environment that is one of the most populer tourist place MahurGad whos Goddess Renuka Devi , which comes in 'Sadetin Pith' of Goddess. Mahur or Mahurgad is a town and religious place in Nanded district of Maharashtra, India.
